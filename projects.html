2015

Tag Cleaner

Built a production-ready MP3 tagging and media authoring tool to extend beyond existing tag editors. The core capability was encoding time-bounded (synchronised) lyrics directly into MP3 files, enabling compatible players and systems to display lyrics in sync with playback. The tool also supported advanced metadata management and album-art editing. Developed for personal use and never publicly released.

Status: Production-ready • Privately used • Never publicly released

2014

Production Support Management

Built a lightweight operational analysis tool to support production support oversight for large, SLA-driven teams.
Consumed CSV exports generated from enterprise ticketing systems (offline ingestion) to analyse workload distribution, activity patterns, and resource availability.
Included Microsoft Outlook integration to enable rapid, custom filtering of operational emails for situational awareness during meetings and reviews.
Used internally during service delivery management for a large-scale enterprise support engagement; discontinued after relocation.

Status: Production-proven • Internally used • Discontinued after role transition

2011

CCM Analyzer

Built a real-time analytics and reporting tool to improve visibility into enterprise support operations during a global platform consolidation.
Integrated with an internal issue-tracking system to provide live insight into ticket status, workflow stage, ownership, and regional distribution.
Enabled weekly operational reporting, team-level performance analysis, and management visibility, contributing to a significant improvement in customer satisfaction over one year.
Later generalised to support offline analysis using CSV-based datasets; discontinued after a role transition.

Status: Production-proven • Internally used • Discontinued after role transition
